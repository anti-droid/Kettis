\problemname{Kettis}

Søren is a big fan of drinking a lot of drinks whenever he parties. Unfortunately this time Søren has been assigned as the groups Designated Driver, but Søren still wants to drink this party. Luckily in Sørens country you are still allowed to drink a little before driving. You must help Søren maximize the number of drinks he can intake and still drive the group home

\section*{Input}
\begin{itemize}
	\item one line with one integer \textit{l}. %INDSÆT BOUNDS HER 
	The legal limit of how many units of alcohol can be intaken while still being allowed to drive
	\item one line with one integer \textit{n}. #INDSÆT BOUNDS HEr
	The number of following drinks
	\item \itemize{n} lines, each defining \textbf{a} drink with a given units of alcohol
\end{itemize}

\section*{Output}
Output \text{M} the maximum amounds of drinks Søren is allowed to intake during the party, while $M \leq l$
